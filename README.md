 # The-Real-Watch
> A watch that ACTUALLY tracks your health by monitoring your surroundings and constantly monitoring for active threats.

---

## What is it and how is it different??

Let me start from the drawing board. I've noticed EXPENSIVE Garmin, Apple and Fitbits that don't even do their real job. Like for example, your health isn't just your BPM, O2 and Pedometer levels. It's more than that. My watch includes ALL those AND a environmental sensor, because the real influencing force is the environment you're in, not how much you walk. This watch constantly scans for CO2, Gas, Noise, Pressure, Noise levels and more! This watch is also capable of running APPs that runs via bluetooth and wifi. It can be used to communicate with other watches eg; if you're hiking and your friend has the same watch. You can communicate with them in realtime like a walkie talkie. This watch isnt just any watch, Its a watch that breaks feats that were never imaginable. Imagine being able to view your bank balance all from your watch that has a ESP32 inside of it!
This is indeed a very ambitious venture but I AM confident I have what it takes.
